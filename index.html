Introduction

Hi my name is Damien Joyce and I am a professionally-qualified personal trainer based in Galway city. If you have just stumbled onto my page or have been referenced onto this site please feel free to drop me a message anytime.


Are you struggling to lose those last few pounds, increase your lifts, tone up or gain in mass I am here for you to help you anyway I can. There is a lot of benefits of regular exercise from stress relief to clearing your head and increasing your cardiovascular endurance. There is therapy in pushing yourself in a safe environment weather that be your home, your local running track or your gym. You can train anywhere with the right mindset.

Online Coaching

I will guide you through your training and make sure your exercise sessions are fully explained. Perfect for anyone looking to push themselves to the next level. Weekly check-ins through video calls and 24/7 contact through WhatsApp or email

Persoal Training

See results with one-to-one exercise sessions. No matter your current fitness level or your current training background, I will make sure you get the best possible advice to help you reach those targets you have set. An exercise and nutrition plan tailored to you.

Health and Nutrition

Don't let food scare or bore you. Your meals should excite you and you should be excited and not feel hungry or guilty for your choices.

Our Goal

It's always good to have a concrete goal in mind. My goal is to create a challenging workout/program that is both FUN and REWARDING! Training can be hard and some days it is harder to find the motivation than others but we here at DJ fitness take it one day at a time. I like to challenge my clients by working on fun and exciting programs on a day to day basis. I am a strong believer in POSITIVE REINFORCEMENT and tough support (much different from negative reinforcements!!) and always operate out of mutual respect.

Our Plan

Is to pass on any knowledege I have when it comes to fitness and nutrition,to keep up to date with our client's on their daily lives and receive constructive feedback on our plans so we can adapt them to best suit our client's needs and goals. I strive to provide excellent customer service as well as support for any fitness related questions or issues that my arise. I have found that tracking your workouts, sleep and nutrition is the best way to reach your goals and it is easily done through our onlline plans that can be accessed from anywhere even your phone.
